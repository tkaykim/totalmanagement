## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

### 1. Introduction
This document outlines the requirements for a web-based ERP solution designed to manage project progress, revenue, and expenses across multiple business units.

### 2. Goals
*   Provide a centralized platform for managing projects and financial data.
*   Enable data-driven decision-making through accurate performance analysis and forecasting.
*   Improve efficiency in financial management and reporting.

### 3. Target Audience
*   Accounting staff
*   Project managers
*   Executives

### 4. Features

#### 4.1. Dashboard
*   **Overview:** Display key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total revenue, total expenses, and net profit.
*   **Business Unit Summary:** Summarize performance by business unit, including active projects, revenue, expenses, and net profit.
*   **Recent Activity:** Integrate with FLOW to display recent tasks and updates.

#### 4.2. Project Management
*   **Project Listing:** Display projects by business unit with key information such as project name, duration, category, revenue, expenses, and net profit.
*   **Project Creation:** Allow users to create new projects with details such as project name, start date, end date, and category.
*Note: 각 사업부의 특성에 맞는 카테고리 설정 기능
*   **Project Details:** Provide a detailed view of each project, including revenue and expense transactions, financial analysis, and progress tracking.

#### 4.3. Revenue and Expense Management
*   **Transaction Tracking:** Track revenue and expense transactions with details such as project, category, amount, and date.
*   **Category Management:** Allow users to define and manage categories for revenue and expenses, specific to each business unit.
*Note: 각 사업부의 특성에 맞는 카테고리 설정 기능
*   **Reporting:** Generate reports for revenue and expenses, filtered by project, business unit, and date range.

#### 4.4. Settlement Management
*   **Revenue Summary:** Display a summary of revenue transactions by project and category.
*   **Expense Summary:** Display a summary of expense transactions by project and category.

#### 4.5. Organization Management
*   **Member Listing:** Display a list of organization members with details such as name, role, and team.

### 5. Use Cases
*   **Monthly Closing:** Generate business unit performance reports at the end of each month.
*   **Budget Management:** Monitor project budget status and make adjustments as needed.
*   **Profitability Analysis:** Analyze the profitability of new projects.

### 6. Technical Requirements
*   **Web-Based:** The solution should be accessible via a web browser.
*   **Responsive Design:** The solution should be responsive and work on different screen sizes.
*   **Database:** A database is required to store project and financial data.
*   **Security:** Secure authentication and authorization mechanisms are required to protect sensitive data.

### 7. Future Considerations
*   **Integration with Accounting Systems:** Integrate with existing accounting systems for seamless data transfer.
*   **Automated Reporting:** Automate the generation of financial reports.
*   **Advanced Analytics:** Implement advanced analytics for forecasting and trend analysis.
